Generated 2026-07-08 from research_nodes-careercombatspread.json, level_thresholds.json, programs/programs/sage/src, and live universe.json. — Kingler for Joseph

HEADLINE FINDING: A balanced 1.5h/day player earns only ~14-22 renown in 30 days under the current curve + 6:1 divisor. The Council Rank tree + 6 career hubs need 46 renown for 100% completion. Even a dedicated 2h/day mixed-player hits only 18 renown in 30 days. Joseph's stated target — "1-2h/day for 30 days → full council rank system" — is mathematically unreachable without curve surgery, divisor reduction, or both. The XP curve's exponential bite (L40 = 177,909 XP) plus the 6:1 renown throttle means a Class-2 player would need ~178 days just to complete the Mining career (28 renown).

1.2 The Renown Mechanic

renown_earned = floor(total_career_levels_gained_across_6_feeders / 6)

The 6 feeders are Pilot, Data Runner, Mining, Building, Crafting, Combat. Daily Check-In is NOT a feeder. Career levels accumulate forever; the divisor carries the remainder forward (so 7 levels = 1 renown + 1 carry).

3.2 What's MISSING from Building vs Mining

Asymmetry alert: Mining has 26 unlocks; Building has 13. Building is missing parallel structure for:
  • Tier-locked specialized buildings — Mining has explicit T1-T5 gear (#119-123); Building's T1-T5 structural complexity (#127-131) only gates processors/hubs/modules, not specialized faction buildings by tier.
  • Risk-zone ladder — Mining has 3 regional extraction tiers (#124-126: faction → enemy → deep space). Building only has Medium Risk (#135). No "High Risk Construction" or "Enemy Zone Construction" counterparts.
  • Hardness analog — Mining gates by resource hardness (#156-159). Building has no equivalent for "extreme surface" or "hostile atmosphere" construction (those are v2 placeholders).
  • "Rare discoveries" endless node — Mining has #82 (Unlimited). Building has no equivalent.

5. Time-to-Completion — the brutal math

Career levels are technically uncapped — the expected_level function in xp.rs:310-344 extrapolates past L40 using the last step size (22,292 XP per additional level). So a player CAN theoretically accumulate enough renown — but the curve past L40 is linear at 22,292 XP/level, which is still brutally steep. Practically:
  • A Class-2 player (1,337 mining XP/hr) would need 16.7 hours of pure mining per extra level past L40.
  • To reach 46 renown (the 100% target) solo via Mining: need 276 career levels. Mining only: 276 × 22,292 = 6.15M XP past L40 = ~4,600 hours of pure mining. That's 8.4 years at 1.5h/day.
  • Across all 6 careers: 276 levels / 6 = 46 levels per career → each career needs ~L46 = ~290K XP. At all-rounder distribution (1,200 XP/day per career) = 242 days.
Bottom line: the hard cap is gone but the soft wall is just as bad. The 6:1 divisor combined with the exponential curve makes 30-day completion mathematically impossible without intervention.

6.3 Recommended combination for 30-day target

Proposal: 4:1 divisor + 1.08 exponential curve + raise level cap to 60.
  • Divisor 4:1 — max renown becomes 60 (6×40÷4), giving headroom for future CR nodes.
  • Curve 1.08 exponential — L40 = 22,810 XP (vs current 177,909). A Class-2 player can hit L40 in one career in ~17 days instead of 89.
  • Cap raised to 60 — gives the "endless" feeling without breaking the curve math; renown max becomes 90.
  • Result: A balanced 1.5h/day player hits ~45 renown in 30 days — enough for Mining + Building careers + most of the CR tree.

7. The "Endless Node" opportunity

#82 Rare Mineral Discoveries is already configured as scalability: Unlimited but has an empty cvc (rare_mineral_discovery: {}). This is the only currently-shippable "endless" node and it's in the Mining career.

Recommended activation pattern:

  • Each level of #82 increases the rare_mineral_multiplier on the character (already a research-derived engine field per folds/consumer.rs).
  • Cost: 1 renown per level (matches existing pricing) OR shift to a separate "endless renown" currency if we want to decouple from the CR tree.
  • Building equivalent: add #136 Master Construction Techniques (Unlimited) — each level reduces construction_time by 2% per rank.

8. Gaps, Risks, and Recommendations

8.1 Critical gaps

  1. Renown hard cap below CR tree cost. 40 max vs 46 needed. Must lower divisor or raise level cap.
  2. Building xp_value is uniform (100) across all 2020 buildings. No incentive to build T4/T5 structures vs T1 spam. Should scale: T1=100, T2=200, T3=400, T4=800, T5=1600 (or similar exponential).
  3. Mining xp_modifier is uniform (1) across all 3283 cargo types. Rare resources should reward more XP. Recommend: tier-scaled (T1=1, T2=2, T3=5, T4=12, T5=30) — rewards mining higher-tier veins.
  4. #117 Siphon Drill is a dead-end node. Per finalize/tables.mjs, classifyDrillBucket routes all fluid/liquid resources to #116 Fluid Extraction, so zero buildings carry the "Siphon Drill -" prefix. Fix: either add members or remove the node.

8.2 Asymmetries between Mining and Building

  • Mining has 26 unlocks; Building has 13. Building needs parallel depth.
  • Building lacks risk-zone ladder (only #135 Medium Risk; no High Risk or Enemy Zone equivalent).
  • Building lacks "hardness" analog (extreme surfaces, hostile atmospheres are v2 placeholders).
  • Building has no "endless node" equivalent of Mining's #82.

8.3 Fun factor assessment

Honest read on "is it fun?": The current shape has two fun-killers:

  1. The first 100 XP grind. A Class-1 starter earning 472 XP/hr needs 12 minutes of pure mining to hit L1 Mining. That's fine. But the next career (say Building) also needs 100 XP, and at 300 XP/hr that's 20 min. With 6 careers all needing L1 to bootstrap, the cold-start is 1-2 hours of varied grinding before the player has any career breadth. Daily Check-In helps but isn't a feeder.
  2. The L20+ wall. Past L20 the curve's exponential bite means each level takes longer than the last in absolute terms but the reward (a research node unlock) stays constant. Diminishing dopamine. Players will disengage around L25-30 when each level takes a week.

What would make it fun: front-load rewards (cheaper early levels), back-load prestige (expensive late levels that grant cosmetic/bragging rights rather than power), and make the "endless" nodes (#82 and a Building equivalent) the long-tail engagement loop with visible per-level effects.

8.4 Recommended action sequence

  1. Chris/Jacob decision: change renown divisor from 6:1 to 4:1 (1-line program change, big progression impact). DEFAULT_RENOWN_DIVISOR in xp.rs:212.
  2. Curve surgery: use the SAGE Editor Suite → Council Rank XP tool to flatten the curve from 1.15 to ~1.08 growth. This is the tool Joseph mentioned.
  3. Per-tier XP scaling: introduce non-uniform xp_value on buildings and xp_modifier on cargo types so higher-tier content rewards proportionally more XP.
  4. Activate #82 as the first real "endless node" with a per-level rare_mineral_multiplier bump, and add a Building equivalent.
  5. Raise level cap from 40 to 60 (or remove it entirely) — converter + XpDefinitions account sizing implications; coordinate with Jacob.
  6. Fix #117 Siphon Drill — either populate members or mark v2.
